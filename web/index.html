Interior Design

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space.

Architecture Design

Architectural Design, also known as AD, is a UK-based architectural journal first launched in 1930 as Architectural Design and Construction. The journal is currently published by John Wiley & Sons, and is edited by Helen Castle since 2001

Exterior Design

Exterior design is the process of designing the exterior of the building. This includes the facade, "skin", roof, and foundational elements. While exterior design can be just as creative and artistic as interior, a knowledge of structural engineering is necessary.
