About me

My name is Maja Urban and I am 27 years old architect from Serbia. My passion is architecture, but I am also very interested in art & design as well as in technical science. I graduated Architecture and Urban Planning at Faculty of Technical Science in Novi Sad

My first experience was on a civil site. There I learned how much complex the realization of project can be. At the same time I was developing some interior design and technical drawings of custom made furniture. As time was passing I concluded that the furniture production could be as complex as the processes on the civil site. In order to keep up with these challenges I am trying to absorb more technical knowledge and experience. But in spite of that, I have to conclude that the thinking process is the more harder. Therefore, after all this great effort, your creation must communicate, inspire, innovate and push the envelope or it won’t be successful.
